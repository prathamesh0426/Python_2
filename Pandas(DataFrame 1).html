Single column is stored as a Series datatype in pandas Dataframe¶

How to get particular element¶

  1. df.iloc[row_index, column_index] index is internal numbering by python
  2. df.loc[row_label, column_label] label is the name provided to row and column headers
In [25]:
df2.iloc[3, 2]
Out[25]:
88.78
In [27]:
df2.loc["S4", "Marks"]
Out[27]:
88.78
